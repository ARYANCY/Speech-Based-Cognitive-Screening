FEATURES EXPLAINED
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

REPETITION RATIO
  What: Words repeated / total words
  Normal: "I went to the store. I bought milk."     → 0.08 (low)
  Dementia: "I like went like to the like store"   → 0.30 (high)

CONTENT WORD RATIO
  What: Content words (nouns/verbs) / total words
  Normal: "I went to the store yesterday"          → 0.70 (high)
  Dementia: "um like I like um went like um"       → 0.30 (low)

AVG WORDS PER SENTENCE
  What: Average sentence length
  Normal: "I went to the store. The weather was nice."  → 6.5 words
  Dementia: "I um like went I like to like the store"  → 3.2 words

SELF-CORRECTION COUNT
  What: "I mean", "that is", "actually", "sorry"
  Normal: "I went to, I mean, I took the train"   → 1 (some awareness)
  Dementia: "I went to the store and forgot why"  → 0 (less aware)

FILLER PER SENTENCE
  What: Total fillers / number of sentences
  Normal: "I went to the store. I bought milk."   → 0 (no fillers)
  Dementia: "I um like went I like um to store"   → 2.5 (many fillers)
